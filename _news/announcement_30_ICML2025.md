[Adnan Mohammed's](/labmembers/) paper, "SparseOpt: Addressing Normalization-induced Gradient Skew in Sparse Training" {% cite adnan2026sparseopt %}, has been accepted at the [International Conference on Machine Learning (ICML), 2026](https://icml.cc/Conferences/2026).
This work introduces SparseOpt, a novel optimization algorithm designed to mitigate the issue of gradient skew caused by normalization in sparse training. SparseOpt effectively balances the gradient updates across all parameters, leading to improved convergence and performance in sparse neural networks.
