This project is a collaboration with <a href="https://www.di.ens.fr/umut.simsekli/">Dr. Umut Simsekli</a> at <a href="http://www.inria.fr/">INRIA - SIERRA</a> team and <a href="https://www.di.ens.fr/">École Normale Supérieure, Computer Science Department</a> in Paris, France.
